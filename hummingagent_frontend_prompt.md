## DESIGN TOKENS

Use a design token approach.

### Radius
- cards: 24px
- buttons: 14px
- pills: full or 999px

### Shadows
Use subtle shadow + inner glow combinations.

### Borders
- 1px rgba white at low opacity
- occasional gradient border accents

### Spacing
Use generous vertical rhythm:
- section padding ~ 96px to 160px desktop
- 64px to 96px tablet
- 48px to 72px mobile
